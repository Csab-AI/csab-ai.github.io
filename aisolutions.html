The Approach

AI tools alone are just raw potential. Without context, they solve nothing. The real value lies in deep process knowledge: identifying the exact pain points where automation translates into reclaimed time and measurable economic gain.

I combine AI technologies creatively and boldly, not just as a developer, but with the perspective of 25 years in management and process improvement. My focus is always on the ROI: shifting from "what can AI do?" to "how does this generate profit?"

1) Process First

Understanding the business logic and pain points before writing a single line of code.

2) Economic Value

Focusing on time savings and efficiency. If it doesn't pay off, it's just a toy.

3) Experience

Leveraging 25 years of cross-industry problem-solving to deploy AI where it actually works.
